Tradition, Service, Friendship

One of the longest serving divisions in Dublin, Walkinstown Drimnagh Castle has a proud history of serving the people of Dublin. Many former members have moved into important roles in St. John Headquarters leaving behind a well structued, motivated and enthusiastic young division eager to build on its traditions. Taking members from a wide catchment area, the Senior and Cadet divisons work cosely leading to a large number of cadets making the successful transition to the senior division. We meet every Friday night from 8-10pm and our trianing covers a wide variety of topics ranging from basic first aid to advanced EMT skills as well as specialist topics.

Our members are very active at both local and national public duties showing off their skill at events as varies as: American Football, Leinster Rugby, Croke Park concerts and matches , Marathons, run, Triatholons and many many more.
